What is Library Method ™?
“Library Method ™ is a structured, human-aware methodology for capturing, classifying, verifying, and retrieving work history as defensible evidence — so you can trust your record and act with clarity.”

Most systems generate activity, not truth. Library Method ™ is built for people and organisations who need provable records: decisions, deliverables, timelines, obligations, accountability, and the underlying narrative that survives pressure.

Purpose

Structure before analysis.
Truth as an asset.

Library Method ™ establishes a disciplined information architecture and verification layer so your history is queryable, portable, and defensible.

The methodology is designed for the realities of modern work: fragmented tooling, distributed teams, regulated environments, and AI systems that amplify whatever you feed them. Integrity is not a feature; it is an operating standard.

Library Method ™ is the foundation for governance-grade personal and organisational records — aligned to evidentiary expectations in the United States and the European Union.

Core principles

Four principles.
One disciplined standard.

Principle 01

No garbage in

Clean inputs create clean outputs. Capture is engineered so error does not become data — and data does not become doctrine.

Principle 02

Structure first

Classification is not overhead; it is the precondition for retrieval, comparison, governance, and meaningful AI assistance.

Principle 03

Truth as an asset

Your record should be provable, not reconstructed. Verification turns work history into defensible evidence.

Principle 04

User ownership

People have the paramount right to govern their own records: access, correction, portability, and evidentiary integrity.

The method

Four stages.
One continuous cycle.

01

Capture

Collect events, decisions, and outputs in your natural workflow — without destroying the signal.

02

Classify

Apply a stable taxonomy: categories, priority, context, and identifiers that survive time and organisational drift.

03

Verify

Make records tamper-evident through integrity checks, provenance, and cryptographic anchors.

04

Retrieve

Surface trusted history instantly — for governance, performance, litigation readiness, and clarity under pressure.

Who it’s for

For those who need
records they can prove.

Professionals, teams, and organisations where accuracy, accountability, and governance matter — and where “close enough” fails.

Founders, product leaders, lawyers, consultants, executives, operators, and anyone who must defend decisions and timelines with evidence. Also: organisations building AI systems that require clean provenance to avoid automated amplification of error.

Library Method ™ is designed for the technical age: truth needs a chain of custody.

Auditable work history, verifiable deliverables, and a defensible record you control.

For teams
